Visit Charlotte, North Carolina

Known as the Queen City, Charlotte has a lot to offer

Top three activities to do in Charlotte

Attend a show or a concert

The Charlotte Theatre is a great place to spend your night out. Popular attractions include Nascar Hall of Fame, The Science Center and more!

Fine wine and dine.

When it comes to revered vineyards that are within reach and a great resume of wine bars, Charlotte's cup runneth over.

Nightlife and Entertainment

Charlotte nightlife is magnetic. When the sun goes down, the city buzzes with music and crafted cocktails.
